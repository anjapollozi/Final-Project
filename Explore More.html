ATV Ride in Reykjanes Peninsula

ATV Riding was one-of-a-kind opportunity to see the mountains, hills and fields of Iceland. On this 2-hour tour of the Reykjanes area, we experienced the scenery that tells the story of Iceland's stark landscape, hewn millennia ago from rivers of magma, riotous rifts, and cooled lava rock.

The tour began with riding between lava fields and black sands heading towards Húsafell, a former farm and church estate, and Fiskidalsfjall mountain, near the birch forest of Húsafellsskógur.

Once we arrived, we reveiled in the panoramic view of the severe but unmistakable dark lava fields, yellow-green moss and multicoloured brush in the Blue Lagoon area. On the return journey, we took in the sea air on a drive along the coastline, where we later made our way to the Blue Lagoon.
